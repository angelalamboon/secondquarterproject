welcome to Angela's Journal

Welcome to my blog! This blog will be about my journal, and it aims to record my progress as a beginner. I'm also striving to expand my creativity as I grow. Journaling was one of the things that I always wanted to try. I am very disorganized, and I have trouble managing time. I would forget many things, like deadlines for my school works or the time for a schedule or meeting. I also love to talk about my day, idols, and interests, and seeing people doing spreads for these things inspired me to try journaling. So here it is! I decided to start my first journal. I hope you have a good time visiting my website! Enjoy!

- ANGELA
